Sesame Miso Yakitori

About The Recipe

Simple and delicious chicken yakitori

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 3 tablespoons red miso
  • 2 tablespoons sake
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds, ground
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1 pound (450 grams) boneless chicken thighs

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, whisk together the soy sauce, mirin, miso, sake (or cacha√ßa), ground sesame seeds, and sugar. Place the saucepan over medium low heat and cook, whisking often, until thickened.

  3. Cut the chicken into 1 inch pieces. Arrange the chicken on the skewers. Grill the skewers, brushing with the glaze from the saucepan.
  4. Cook until golden, then turn and brush again with the miso glaze. Continue to cook until the chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Brush the cooked chicken skewers with the clean brush and bowl of miso glaze.
